Alex moves through the city like someone who has learned to take up less space.
The route is mechanical, the face neutral, tips pocketed without eye contact.
A person who became very good at something and used that skill to disappear.

Note what Alex doesn't do — doesn't look at faces, doesn't linger, doesn't ask
questions. That discipline has a history we haven't revealed yet.
