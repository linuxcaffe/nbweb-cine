The lowest point. Everything the protagonist was counting on is gone.
The external problem is unsolvable. The internal wound is fully exposed.

VALE takes the message. Cass disappears. Alex is alone in a shuttered
building on the east side with nothing left to deliver and nowhere to go.

---
**Craft note — All Is Lost (~75%):** The whiff of death belongs here — literal or
metaphorical. Something must die: a relationship, a belief, a version of the
protagonist. The external loss creates the opening for the internal reckoning
that makes Act 3 possible. If the protagonist hasn't changed internally by the
end of this beat, Act 3 will feel mechanical. The Dark Night of the Soul scene
that follows is where that change begins.
