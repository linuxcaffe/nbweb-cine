An empty building on the east side. Alex sits with nothing.

What surfaces in the silence is not the present problem but the older one.
The delivery eleven years ago that didn't happen. The person who didn't get
what they needed. The reason Alex took jobs that didn't matter — so nothing
could go wrong in a way that mattered.
