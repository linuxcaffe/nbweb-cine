The door opens. Alex says the words out loud instead of handing anything over.
There's nothing to hand over — VALE has the envelope. The confession was never
on paper. It was in Alex's mouth the whole time.

The person at the door: I know. Voss told me you'd come.

---
**Craft note — Resolution of External Plot:** The MacGuffin is revealed as a
MacGuffin — the real delivery was something else. This works only if the audience
has been given the information to recognize it as inevitable in retrospect. Seed
it early (the weight of the envelope, something VOSS said, the handwriting) so
the reveal feels discovered rather than invented. Earned surprise vs. cheap twist:
the difference is what was there all along.
