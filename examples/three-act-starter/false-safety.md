The bridge was the choke point. They made it. Open water on both sides,
lights of the far bank ahead. Cass is laughing — not at anything, just
at the fact of still moving.

Alex feels it too. Something that might be joy, or might be relief
mistaken for joy.

---
**Craft note — Post-Midpoint beat:** This is the scene that follows the Midpoint
marker. It must be genuine enough that the audience believes it. The fall at
All Is Lost lands proportionally harder the more fully the audience believed
the false peak. Write the relief as if it were real, because for this moment,
it is.
