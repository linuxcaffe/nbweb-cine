The protagonist commits — or is forced to commit — to the central action.
The ordinary world is gone. There is no going back.

VALE knows Alex's apartment. Alex sees the light on through the window from
the street below — the light Alex left off. Dropping the package is no longer
an option. Alex turns away from the building and keeps moving.

---
**Craft note — First Plot Point (~25%):** This is the true beginning of Act 2.
Everything before it was setup. Notice that Alex commits for external reasons —
not because they've chosen the quest but because the exit has been taken away.
This is intentional: the internal choice comes much later, at the Second Plot
Point. External pressure forces action; internal change is what makes it matter.
