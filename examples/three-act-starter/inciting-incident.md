The call that cracks the ordinary world open. Not a small disruption — a thing
that cannot be ignored, cannot be unfelt.

Alex sees VOSS's seal on the envelope. VOSS is supposed to be dead. The payment
is three times the going rate. The city shuts down in four hours.

---
**Craft note — Inciting Incident:** This happens around the 10% mark. The protagonist
doesn't have to commit here — they can resist, hedge, delay. What matters is that
the ordinary world is no longer sufficient. Something has entered it that doesn't
belong. The protagonist has been disturbed. Act 1's remaining beats are about the
disturbance deepening until the First Plot Point forces commitment.
