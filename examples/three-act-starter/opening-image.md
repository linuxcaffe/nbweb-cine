The city before the story begins. Whatever will change hasn't changed yet.

Alex on the platform at 3am. The last train pulling away. The city shutting
down for the night. Familiar route, familiar indifference. A person who has
learned to take up very little space.

---
**Craft note — Opening Image:** The opening image and the final image are mirrors.
What is different between them IS the movie. Plant the visual grammar here —
the platform, the city, the hour, the quality of Alex's stillness — so that
when it reappears transformed in the Resolution, the audience feels it without
being told what to feel.
