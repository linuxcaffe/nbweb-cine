A new piece of information, a decision, or a gift from an ally that makes
Act 3 possible. The protagonist reorients — not rescued, but refocused.

Alex sits with nothing. And then remembers something VOSS said, years ago,
about what a delivery actually is. The address was never the destination.
The message was always a spoken thing.

---
**Craft note — Second Plot Point (~75-80%):** This is not a deus ex machina — it
must be seeded earlier. The information Alex recalls should have been present in
Act 1 or Act 2A, unremarkable at the time. The protagonist finds the way back
not through luck but through understanding something they already had. The
difference between this and a convenient rescue is that the protagonist does
the work themselves.
