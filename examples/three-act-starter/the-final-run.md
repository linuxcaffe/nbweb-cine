Alex knows something VALE doesn't: the address was never the destination.
The message was always going to be spoken. VOSS knew that. Alex finally
knows it too.

One block. VALE is one step behind. This is a race, but simpler — it's
just moving toward instead of away.

---
**Craft note — Act 3 Drive to Climax:** The protagonist's movement now expresses
their internal change. Alex is not running from VALE — Alex is running toward
something. That distinction is everything. The physical action of Act 3 should
feel qualitatively different from Act 2, even if the surface events look similar,
because the internal orientation has changed.
