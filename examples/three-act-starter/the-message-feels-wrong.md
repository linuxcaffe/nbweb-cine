Professional couriers don't open packages. Alex knows this. But the weight is
wrong, and the handwriting on the address is VOSS's. VOSS who is supposed to
be dead.

Alex doesn't open it. But carries something that belongs to a dead person
through a darkening city.

---
**Craft note:** The mystery of the package gives the audience something to want
(they want to know what's inside) which keeps them wanting Alex to keep moving.
The MacGuffin doesn't need to be interesting in itself — only the wanting needs
to be interesting. Resist explaining. Let the weight be wrong and leave it there.
