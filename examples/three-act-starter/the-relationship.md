The B-plot. CASS appears midway through Act 2A — claims to have known the sender,
offers help, gives no reason to trust them. The subplot asks: who is Cass, and
what does it cost to trust someone you can't verify?

The subplot resolves in parallel with the main plot. Cass's final choice mirrors
Alex's transformation, making the two characters reflections of each other.
